Getting Started

Creating Your First Lesson

  1. Open the MemQ app and tap the "Create" button
  2. Enter a name for your lesson (e.g., "Biology Chapter 1")
  3. Optionally add a description and set a deadline
  4. Tap "Create" to save your lesson
  5. Start adding terms to your lesson manually, or use AI to generate them

Adding Terms to Your Lesson

There are several ways to add terms:

  • Manual Entry: Tap "Add Term" and enter the term and definition yourself
  • AI Generation: Use the AI assistant to generate terms from a topic or conversation
  • PDF Upload: Upload a PDF document and let AI extract key terms automatically
  • From Topic: Provide a topic and let AI generate relevant terms and definitions

Studying with Flashcards

  1. Navigate to your lesson and tap "Study"
  2. Review the term on the front of the card
  3. Tap the card to flip it and see the definition
  4. Rate your knowledge level (New, Learning, Familiar, Good, Strong, Mastered)
  5. The app will schedule future reviews based on spaced repetition

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI assistant work? ‚ñº
The AI assistant uses advanced language models to help you learn. You can ask questions about your study materials, request explanations, generate terms from topics, or extract key concepts from conversations. The AI is designed to provide educational support and enhance your learning experience.
What is spaced repetition? ‚ñº
Spaced repetition is a learning technique that involves reviewing material at increasing intervals over time. MemQ uses intelligent algorithms to determine when you should review each term based on your performance. Terms you struggle with appear more frequently, while mastered terms are reviewed less often, optimizing your study time.
Can I upload PDF documents? ‚ñº
Yes! You can upload PDF documents through the AI assistant. The app will analyze the document and automatically extract key terms and concepts, creating flashcards for you. This is especially useful for textbooks, research papers, or study guides.
How do I track my progress? ‚ñº
MemQ tracks your progress automatically. On the home screen, you'll see your daily goal and progress. Each lesson shows completion statistics, and you can view detailed progress for individual terms. The app tracks which terms you've mastered and which need more review.
What are the different learning stages? ‚ñº
Terms progress through six stages: New (just added), Learning (first attempts), Familiar (getting comfortable), Good (solid understanding), Strong (well-known), and Mastered (fully learned). The app adjusts review frequency based on these stages.
How do subscriptions work? ‚ñº
MemQ offers subscription plans that unlock premium features. Subscriptions are managed through your device's App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android). You can cancel anytime through your device settings. Subscriptions automatically renew unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the billing period.
Can I delete my account? ‚ñº
Yes, you can delete your account at any time through the Profile settings in the app. When you delete your account, all your data including lessons, terms, progress, and conversations will be permanently removed. This action cannot be undone.
Is my data secure? ‚ñº
Yes, we take data security seriously. All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS. We use secure authentication and access controls. Your data is stored securely using industry-standard practices. For more details, please see our Privacy Policy.
