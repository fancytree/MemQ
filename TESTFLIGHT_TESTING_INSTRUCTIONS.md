# TestFlight Testing Instructions

## What to Test

Thank you for participating in the Beta test! Please focus on testing the following features:

### 1. Authentication & Account Setup
- ✅ Sign up with email
- ✅ Sign in with email, Google, or Apple
- ✅ Password reset functionality
- ✅ Account creation and profile setup

### 2. Course Management
- ✅ Create a new course
- ✅ Add terms and definitions manually
- ✅ Edit and delete terms
- ✅ Organize content by courses

### 3. AI Features
- ✅ Generate terms from text using AI
- ✅ Generate quiz questions from your content
- ✅ Use AI chat assistant for learning help
- ✅ Test all three chat modes: Practice, Ask, and Vocabulary Lookup

### 4. PDF Import
- ✅ Upload PDF documents
- ✅ Verify that content is extracted correctly
- ✅ Check if terms are automatically created

### 5. Learning Features
- ✅ Start a flashcard study session
- ✅ Test the spaced repetition algorithm
- ✅ Track your learning progress
- ✅ Review mastered terms

### 6. Subscription & Payments
- ✅ View the paywall
- ✅ Test subscription purchase flow (use Sandbox account)
- ✅ Verify subscription status updates
- ✅ Test restore purchases functionality

### 7. Notifications
- ✅ Enable study reminders in Settings
- ✅ Set custom reminder times
- ✅ Verify notifications are received at scheduled times
- ✅ Test notification permissions

### 8. General App Experience
- ✅ Navigation between screens
- ✅ App performance and responsiveness
- ✅ UI/UX feedback
- ✅ Any crashes or unexpected behavior

## How to Report Issues

If you encounter any problems or have suggestions:

1. **Use TestFlight Feedback**: Tap the "Send Feedback" button in TestFlight
2. **Email**: Send detailed reports to support@riverstudio.cc
   - Include: Device model, iOS version, steps to reproduce, screenshots if possible

## What We're Looking For

- **Bugs**: Any crashes, errors, or unexpected behavior
- **Usability**: Is the app easy to use? Are there confusing parts?
- **Performance**: Does the app feel fast and responsive?
- **Feature Requests**: What would make the app better?

## Thank You!

Your feedback is invaluable in helping us improve MemQ. We appreciate your time and input!
